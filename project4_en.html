FACE MASKS & RESPIRATORS

Surgical masks and respirators, made of specialty materials, that afford a high level of respiratory protection LAST UPDATE 03/04/2020 ΔΙΑΒΑΣΤΕ ΣΤΑ ΕΛΛΗΝΙΚΑ

PROJECT OBJECTIVE


  • Designing of solutions to substitute surgical masks that can be immediately produced at a mass rate.
  • Open and accessible designs, free of charge, under Creative Commons copyright licenses (4.0 International License) Attribution-NonCommercial terms.
  • Enabling the commercial exploitation by potential manufacturers, under the condition of fair pricing (preclusion of profiteering).
  • Providing technical support towards potential manufacturers to assist in concurrent production at multiple regions of Greece.
  • Coordination with medical associations and hospitals in order to meet the needs of frontline health professionals as soon as possible.

CURRENT NEEDS


Please contact Covid-19 Response Greece if you have any knowledge of (a) controlling the performance of materials used in filters, (b) materials or the ability to find - provide materials to perform quality controls regarding the individual properties of filters (particle counter) and (c) people who have direct or indirect contact with industries producing antibacterial filters or artisanships that procure them. (d) a person who has the academic background to study some research on the use of alternative materials as filters, such as zeolite.

FREE AVAILABILITY OF DESIGNS


  • All the designs, created by Covid-19 Response volunteers, in the scope of this action, are provided free of charge, under Creative Commons (4.0 International License) Attribution-NonCommercial terms, as many voluntary actions do worldwide..
  • The designs are available to any interested party in order to proceed with the manufacturing of protective face shields which, in turn, must be provided to healthcare professionals free of charge, in coordination with our volunteering team and the Panhellenic Medical Association.. It is specifically requested to prioritize hospitals which need them the most.
  • In order to avoid profiteering, Covid-19 Response Greece forbids commercial exploitation of its designs For any business that cannot afford to produce and provide the protective face shields free of charge, special terms of use can be granted that allow the production of the shields at an agreed upon, reasonable price that covers the cost of production, by contacting us at contact@covid19response.gr in order to accuire a licensing agreement.
