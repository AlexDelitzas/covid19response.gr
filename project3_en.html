Online Self-Assessment

Online self-assessment & remote monitoring system for people who have potential COVID-19 symptoms LAST UPDATE: 09/04/2020 διαβαστε στα ελληνικα

Project Goal


  • Creation and distribution of online self-assessment and self-monitoring tools in order to:
    • Reduce the amount of calls to the emergency line of the Hellenic National Public Health Organization (HNPHO) 1135
    • Achieve immediate cooperation with competent authorities and provide assistance in implementing software solutions to manage the pandemic
  • Development of open-source software, accessible to competent authorities, government services and healthcare monitoring services, free of charge under the terms of Creative Commons (4.0 International License) Attribution-NonCommercial;/li>
  • To provide technical support and coordinate with interested parties to develop and modify software in the scope of monitoring and managing the pandemic

1. FORM DRAFTING


The self-assessment form is based on the equivalent form published by the health authorities of Alberta, Canada. [COVID Self Assessment]. This specific questionnaire was translated into Greek and modified accordingly.

In this form, the user is requested to answer certain questions and, depending on the answers, is asked to provide further clarifications. The system gathers necessary information in each step of the process and then suggests necessary actions. These actions may vary from self-management of the disease to seeking immediate assistance at a suggested hospital.

Our goal is self-management and traffic reduction of the HNPHO emergency line 1135.

1. SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT


The software developed by Covid-19 Response Greece records the number and intensity of the user’s symptoms in order to:

  1. Inform the user of appropriate actions.
  2. Establish a future timeframe in which to contact the user, re-evaluate their symptoms and, once again, suggestion an appropriate course of action.

Our goal is to enable each user to self-manage their symptoms so the traffic of the emergency line 1135 is reduced.

1. TRANSLATION AND CUSTOMIZATION OF TRACKING APPLICATIONS


The Covid-19 Response Greece team is studying tracking applications suggested by other states, the analysis of their functionality, as well as the potential for their translation and adaptation for greek standards.

  1. Shield/Hamagen - Israel

    This app monitors the movement of the user in real-time using the location services of his/her phone. The information is stored locally on the user’s device and, at the same time, the application informs them whether they have been in close proximity to a confirmed case and suggests necessary actions. The relevant information is also updated by the nursing staff based on the confirmed cases that are hospitalized.

    In addition, the user can voluntarily share the locations they have recently been to with the Ministry of Health or the NPHO, so that if they are proven to be a confirmed case of the virus later, they can inform other users in real time who have been in close proximity with them.

    • The app is available here.
    • The open-source code is available here.
  2. TraceTogether - Singapore

    This app monitors the movement of the user in real-time using the proximity of his/her device with another (via bluetooth). The information is stored locally on the user’s device and, at the same time, the application informs them whether they have been in close proximity to a confirmed case.

    In addition, the user can voluntarily share the locations they have recently been to with the Ministry of Health or the NPHO, so that if they are proven to be a confirmed case of the virus later, they can inform other users in real time who have been in close proximity with them.

    • The app is available here.
