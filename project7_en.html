PSYCHOLOGY WEBINARS


Our Webinars are carefully tailored to the ongoing needs of the wider public, especially in this time of mental and health crisis. Each webinar is organised and reviewed by a team of psychologists, psychiatrists and other mental health professionals. Our aim is twofold: 1. give people the opportunity to express themselves and 2. provide helpful advice for personal growth and emotional wellbeing. ΔΙΑΒΑΣΤΕ ΣΤΑ ΕΛΛΗΝΙΚΑ

PROJECT TARGET


  • Mental health and wellbeing are issues that concern every single one of us, especially in this health crisis caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. Everyone faces an increased number of worries that eventually put a strain on our coping resources. This highlights the need to identify novel ways of managing our thoughts, emotions and overall experiences. In these webinar series we propose ways to increase emotional wellbeing in our daily life using an interactive and human-centered approach.
  • The target of this project is to provide everyone with useful advice to manage potential predicaments. Most importantly, we aim to offer a safe and welcoming space for expression and constructive discussion on topics that concern all of us. Our mission statement can be narrowed down into one sentence: “We do stay at home but we come out better” by turning this crisis into a unique opportunity of self-exploration and personal growth.

Webinars Categories


We offer eight (8) different categories of online seminars related to eight (8) different emerging fields, that become increasingly important in this time of quarantine and/or isolation. You may find the Webinars that correspond better to your own needs and questions according to the following “coloured” categories:

Α) Coral Series - Tools to Manage Stress

Through this category of webinars, you can find advice and tools to manage potential thoughts and emotions that can cause stress and poor quality of life. Based on short-term therapeutic models and psychological first aid kits, online coral seminars may help the attendee to navigate change better, especially in cases of pressure or stress.

B) Purple Series - Relationships balance / Communication

In the purple-series of webinars, we bring at the forefront personal relationships,which can range from companionships, family relationships, friendships to every other kind of human bonding. Through the purple-webinars attendees we will learn how to manage our relationships better, communicate more effectively and help our peers and ourselves to create meaningful bonds and interactions. Our team aims in providing solutions to every-day relationship problems with the upper goal to achieve a healthy balance on our interactions with significant others family and friends. In this way we can listen to each other and express ourselves in a more constructive and productive manner.

C) Teal Series - Domestic Violence/ Manage Crisis and Conflict

Teal series webinars address the increasingly worrying phenomenon of domestic violence. During this pandemic, domestic violence has become even more frequent, potentially due to the increased number of conflicts between family members. We refer to situations of increasing tension, conflict, and cases of verbal, physical or psychological violence and abuse. We aim to provide a background on the underlying reasons that might instigate domestic violence of any kind and advice on how to manage these circumstances. We additionally aim in providing various sources; “Remember, you are not alone”!

D) Orange Series- Frontliners support and special issues

Are you working in the frontline during the pandemic crisis? Are you a nurse, a supermarket employee, a doctor, a police officer? Then this webinar series is for you! Everybody, who helps the public to stay home safe, face a vast amount of challenges that can lead to emotional and physical exhaustion. A special category of online series will encompass suggestions and coping techniques, which will boost and facilitate your emotional wellbeing during this health crisis.

E) Yellow Series - Caregivers Support

Caregivers who support and assist people in need (e.g., elderly people, children with special needs, people with mobility and autonomy problems) are constantly found in conditions of increasing stress, tiredness and burnout. In this health crisis, the need for care increases proportionally to ongoing challenges. Yellow series webinars address all the issues related to caregiving within a pandemic context, with the final goal helping caregivers to manage better contemporary demands.

F) MarineSeries - Sadness and inaction

Being isolated due to lockdown and shelter in place orders may vastly affect individuals mood and motivation; for example decreased energy and lack of pleasure has been widely reported. Here we discuss the psychological impact of self-isolating and the mental toll of slashing time spent socializing. Since we do not know yet how much time this situation will last, we created these series to navigate change together. In marine series webinars, you can find advice, tools and solutions, to take control of your mood and to find your lost energy.

G) Pink Series - Special challenges and conditions covid-19

Without a doubt covid-19 pandemic crisis was beyond our imagination and definitely we were not prepared for. Many things have changed during a short period of time in a way that we did not anticipate. In the pink series webinars we discover together how we can adapt to this new life perspective and discuss the current challenges. A vast focus of these series will be attributed to professional and economic changes, e.g., working from home and economical insecurities accordingly.

Η) Mint Series - Relax and Wellness

Last but not least, during this pandemic, we should take care of both our body and mind as a unit. During the mint series, you may attend online seminars, which will provide relaxation and wellness techniques, daily tools, such as mindfulness meditation, you may use to improve your physical and psychological well being.
