PSYCHOLOGY WEBINARS


Μενουμε σπιτι, βγαινουμε καλυτεροι

Webinars for public by psychologists,psychiatrists and other scientists of mental health and wellness which can help everyone to feel better.

PROJECT TARGET


  • Mental health and wellness are issues that concern everyone, especially in these life circumstances which we undergo due to pandemic of Covid-19. Everyone faces more difficulties now, that they test our strength and the ways to manage our wellness,the feeling of health and pleasure in daily life
  • Project target is to serve everyone useful advices, tips,tools to manage stress and a space of expression and discussion, for all these subjects that concern us. So, we stay home and come out better, turning this crisis into an opportunity.

Webinars Categories


8 different categories of online seminars , for 8 different recent needs. Find your own and follow according to colour.

Α) Coral Series - Tools to Manage Stress

Through the webinar, you may find advices and tools to manage the thoughts and the factors which cause stress. Based on short term therapeutic models and psychological first aid, online coral seminars may help you feel better, in case you feel pressure or stress.

B) Purple Series - Relationships balance / Communication

In purple series webinar we analyze personal relationships and their balance. Companionships, family relationships, friendships and all human relationships. Through the webinars you will learn how to manage your relations , communicate more effectively, helping the others and yourself feeling a positive experience of bonding and interaction. Find solutions to problems and create a better and more healthy balance

C) Teal Series - Domestic Violence/ Manage Crisis and Conflict

Teal series webinars address to an increasing difficult situation, which may have been existed, but often get worst in conditions of increasing interaction between family members. We refer to situations of increasing tension, conflict, and cases of verbal, physical or psychological violence. Learn how to manage these circumstances ,decrease tension or search the adequate support from your place. In these special circumstances you are not alone!

D) Orange Series - Frontliners support and special issues

Are you working in the frontline during the pandemic crisis? Are you a nurse,a supermarket employee, a doctor, a police officer? Then this webinar series is for you! Facing these special circumstances,daily insecurity and fear for your own and your family's health, everybody that helps the public to stay home safe , you deserve an online series made just for you to help cope better with the increased challenges.

E) Yellow Series - Caregivers Support

The caregivers who support and care people with increasing needs, elderly people, hildren with special needs, people with mobility and autonomy problems, which are constantly found in conditions of increasing stress and tiredness. In these circumstances we undergo, the need of care increases and new challenges occur, which we couldn't expect before. Yellow series webinars address to all the issues and needs mentioned above, helping caregivers to manage better the contemporary demands.

F) MarineSeries - Sadness and inaction

Affect the individuals biologically, decreasing the energy, the joy, the pleasure and the willingness to act. If someone remains in this situation, it can be last for a long time. We have good news! In marine series webinars, you can find advices, tools and solutions, to take control of their mood and to find their lost energy.

G) Pink Series - Ειδικές προκλήσεις και συνθήκες Covid-19

For sure Covid-19 pandemic crisis was not something that we could imagine, or something we were not prepared for. Many things have changed during a short period of time in a way which we did not have the chance to be prepared for. In the pink series webinars we discover new perspectives and ways for brand new challenges and changes , for instance working from home and economical insecurities.

Η) Mint Series - Relax and Wellness

Last but not least, in these circumstances, we should take care of our body and its wellness. Hence, soul and body are a unit. During the mint series, you may attend online seminars, which will provide relaxing and wellness techniques, daily tools to take care of you , your body and your physical and psychological health.
