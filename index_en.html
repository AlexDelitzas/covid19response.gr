WAYS YOU CAN HELP COVID-19 RESPONSE GREECE


  • If you belong in a competent authority or an entity of the health sector, you can help us by providing information & suggestions in relation to crucial needs and problems so as to focus our efforts accordingly.
  • If you are an expert in medical issues, a scientist or an engineer, production specialist & supply chain manager, you can provide us with your expertise, counseling and access to logistics infrastructure.
  • If you are an individual, a business or an entity which does not belong to any of the above mentioned categories, you can support the effort by offering time, recommendations and/or financial support to cover the costs of production and distribution.
