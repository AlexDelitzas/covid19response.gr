CPAP / NIV Helmet

NON-INVASIVE MECHANICAL VENTILATION EQUIPMENT FOR HOSPITALIZED PATIENTS LAST UPDATE: 31/03/2020 διαβστε στα ελληνικα

PROJECT OBJECTIVE


  • Solution designing that can function as substitutes for non-invasive mechanical ventilation equipment or/and their specific components.
  • Open and accessible designs, free of charge, under Creative Commons copyright licenses (4.0 International License) Attribution-NonCommercial terms.
  • Enabling the commercial exploitation by potential manufacturers, under the condition of fair pricing (preclusion of profiteering).
  • Providing technical support towards potential manufacturers to assist in concurrent production at multiple regions of Greece.
  • Coordination with medical associations and hospitals in order to meet the needs of front line health professionals as soon as possible.

DEVELOPMENT STAGE


NEEDS ANALYSIS
RESEARCH
DESIGN
PRODUCTION

CURRENT NEEDS


  • Please contact us if you have any knowledge of (a) the functioning of non-invasive mechanical ventilation equipment, (b) the designing and manufacturing of individual components of the equipment, and (c) the availability of those individual components in the market.
  • Specifically, the Covid-19 Response Greece team is seeking medical staff or/and designers/engineers with relevant knowledge about setting requirements and designing possible solutions.

COORDINATION & CONTACT


  • Email: contact@covid19response.gr
  • Point of contact for the project: Stefanos Tsiakmakis, Project Helmet Coordinator, Voluntary Action COVID-19 Response Greece
  • Point of contact for other inquiries: Dimitrios Kourtesis, Coordinator, Voluntary Action COVID-19 Response Greece

FREE AVAILABILITY OF DESIGNS


  • All the designs, created by Covid-19 Response Greece volunteers in the scope of this action, are provided free of charge, under the Creative Commons (4.0 International License) Attribution-NonCommercial terms, as many voluntary actions do worldwide.
  • The designs are available to any interested party in order to proceed with the manufacturing of protective face shields, which, in turn, must be provided to healthcare professionals free of charge, in coordination with our volunteering team and the Panhellenic Medical Association. It is specifically requested to prioritize hospitals which need them the most.
  • In order to avoid profiteering, Covid-19 Response Greece forbids commercial exploitation of its designs. or any business that cannot afford to produce and provide the protective face shields free of charge, special terms of use can be granted that allow the production of the shields at an ,agreed upon, reasonable price that covers the cost of production, by contacting us at contact@covid19response.gr.
  • The designs presented below are emergency solutions, driven by the lack of personal protective equipment that the outbreak has caused. No product has been certified and, thus, may not be suitable for the needs of certain medical specialties that have high protection requirements.
